Meet the Creator

Hi, I'm Lily—a metro Detroit native and an advertising student at OCAD University. Hailing from the Motor City, my design journey is an homage to my roots, drawing inspiration from urban culture, the art of storytelling, and resilient spirit.

In my creative pursuits, I boldly challenge conventions, pushing boundaries to unveil the true extent of my artistic expression. As a passionate advocate for diverse perspectives, I see the world as a canvas painted with myriad hues waiting to be explored. I transform ideas into visual stories and embrace a vivid and dynamic approach to design, infusing each project with a playful burst of vibrant colors, because what’s the fun of living in black and white?
